How my productivity timer works:

The break button starts the break timer (in red) and would stop the red timer. The work timer does the same thing, in reverse.
This is designed so when you are doing work, you press the 'work' button and the timer will track the time you work for.
As soon as you stop working, press the 'break' button and then the break timer will start. This will encourage you to get back to work.

The timing data is stored on your computer, so even if you close the tab, your time will be saved.
